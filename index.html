Me

I am a non-binary spirit strolling through life by encompassing visual experiences and threading them into stories, films, and analogue photographs. I strive for connection and evolution, change and exploration. When I create art, I enable myself and others to go deeper into the human experience. I don't know how not to do that, that's how I know I'm on the right path.

My Hobby

I am mostly interested in analogue photography because it offer a more interesting process. It is less goal oriented and because of it's nature itself, it helps me with the immersion. It is abstract yet grounding at the same time.

Film Photography

Analog photography, also known as film photography, is a catch-all term for photography that uses chemical processes to capture an image, typically on paper, film or a hard plate. These analog processes were the only methods available to photographers for more than a century prior to the invention of digital photography, which uses electronic sensors to record images to digital media.
